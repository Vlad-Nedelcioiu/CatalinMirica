# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

People planning events across the full mix — engaged couples, corporate and event organizers, families and individuals booking portrait sessions. No single client type dominates. They typically arrive warm: referrals and social media drive leads, so most visitors already have a reason to be curious. Their job here is to confirm the studio is as good as they've heard, understand whether it fits *their* kind of event, and find a low-pressure way to start a conversation.

## Product Purpose

The public website for Timeless Visuals, an events photography & videography studio. It exists to close leads that originate elsewhere: the portfolio convinces, the services and packages show fit, and the contact/booking flows make the next step easy. Success is credibility — a visitor leaves convinced of the studio's eye and professionalism, and reaches out. A lightweight internal admin dashboard (`/admin`) lists bookings and messages; it stays functional but is explicitly secondary.

## Positioning

A calm, cinematic eye across every kind of event — the studio whose own website proves the taste it sells.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: **View portfolio** — lead visitors into the work first; the work does the selling.
- Secondary CTA: **Get in touch / Book a session** — the natural next step once convinced; low-pressure, transparent packages.
- The line a visitor remembers after 10 seconds: **"Where moments become memories."**
- Belief ladder:
  1. "Their work is genuinely beautiful" — portfolio does the talking
  2. "They can handle MY kind of event" — breadth across weddings, corporate, portraits
  3. "They're reliable professionals" — process, testimonials, years active
  4. "Reaching out is easy and low-pressure" — clear contact, transparent packages
- Proof on hand: **mostly placeholder.** The current testimonials, review counts ("5.0 across 180+ reviews"), and stats are scaffold copy. Real testimonials and figures must be collected and swapped in before launch; invented proof must never ship. Collect assets into `.impeccable/assets/proof/` as they arrive.

## Brand Personality

Calm, editorial, timeless. Unhurried and refined — the site lets the photography breathe and earns confidence through restraint rather than volume. The voice is a studio presenting its way of seeing, not a vendor pitching. Emotional goals: quiet confidence, warmth without sentimentality, the sense that this work will still look good in twenty years.

## Anti-references

- **Generic template look** — interchangeable agency/SaaS landing page: stock sections, icon grids, could be anyone's site.
- **Cheesy wedding-vendor** — script fonts, rose gold, heart motifs, sentimental clip-art aesthetics.
- **Dark moody luxury** — near-black backgrounds, gold serif, exclusive-hotel vibes.
- **Trendy / maximalist** — loud gradients, oversized type experiments, brutalist or acid aesthetics that date quickly.

## Design Principles

1. **The work does the talking.** Layout, chrome, and copy defer to the photography; the site is the portfolio's frame, never its competitor.
2. **The site is itself the proof.** A studio selling taste must demonstrate taste — every detail of the site's craft is a credibility signal.
3. **Calm confidence, never salesmanship.** No urgency tricks, no hard sells. Unhurried pacing and transparent packages are the persuasion.
4. **Breadth without dilution.** Show range across weddings, corporate, concerts, and portraits while keeping one coherent voice.
5. **Never fake the proof.** Placeholder testimonials and stats are scaffolding, not content — nothing invented ships as real.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.2 AA: ≥4.5:1 text contrast (3:1 for large text), full keyboard navigation, visible focus states, and `prefers-reduced-motion` alternatives for all animation (a global reduced-motion rule already exists in `app/globals.css`).
